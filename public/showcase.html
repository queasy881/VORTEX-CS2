Bone-Scanning Aimbot

External Memory Read

Unlike traditional FOV checks that only look at one bone, VORTEX scans 15 key bones per player — head, neck, spine, pelvis, shoulders, hands, knees, and feet. If any part of the enemy's body enters your FOV circle, they become a valid target. You aim at your selected hitbox (head, neck, chest, body) while the FOV check is against the nearest visible bone.

Two modes: smooth tracking with configurable smoothness (1-30), or instant flick. Multi-hitbox fallback chains let you set primary → secondary → tertiary hitboxes so if the head isn't reachable, it falls back to chest automatically.

15 bones scanned smooth / flick multi-hitbox FOV 1-30° lock delay priority: FOV / dist / HP

Full ESP Suite

External Injected DLL

Hybrid ESP — the external overlay handles radar, crosshair, FOV circle, and HUD elements, while an optional injected DLL renders in-game ESP via D3D11 for pixel-perfect player overlays. Box ESP supports full or corner styles with adjustable thickness. Health bars can be positioned left, right, or bottom with dynamic color gradients (green to red).

Every element is individually toggleable and color-customizable through the visual color picker with a hue bar and 16 preset swatches. Skeleton rendering shows 7 bone connections with optional joint dots. Distance-based snaplines with dashed/solid styles.

Direct2D Menu

HWND Safe DirectComposition

960×560 transparent overlay rendered with Direct2D and DirectComposition — true per-pixel alpha with no sharp window rectangle visible. The menu runs on its own thread and doesn't touch the game process at all. Segoe UI typography throughout, with 5 tabs (Combat, Visuals, Misc, HUD, Config) and scrollable cards with clipping.

Color pickers feature a full hue bar with 16 preset swatches and fine-tune RGB sliders. 7 accent themes (red, blue, purple, green, orange, pink, cyan) change the entire menu color scheme instantly. The ESP preview card shows a live rendering of your current settings before you even enter a match.

960×560 per-pixel alpha 7 themes hue bar picker ESP preview scrollable cards keybind capture

Triggerbot

External SendInput

Fires automatically when your crosshair is on an enemy. Head-only mode uses real bone intersection — it reads the crosshair entity, resolves the pawn, gets the head bone position, and only fires if you're within 2.5° of the actual head. Not a simplified hitgroup check.

Configurable delay randomization (min/max ms) makes timing human-like. Burst mode limits shots per trigger hold. Auto-stop writes to movement force values to stop your character before firing for maximum accuracy.

head-only mode delay randomization burst limit auto-stop bone intersection

Config System

Local Save

Multi-config file browser — all configs saved as individual .cfg files in %LOCALAPPDATA%\cs2_cfg\. Create, load, save, and delete named profiles from the menu. A competitive preset resets everything then applies a clean tournament-safe profile: aimbot on head with FOV 3 and smooth 5, box ESP with health and names, radar, watermark, and hit indicators.

Every action triggers a toast notification at the bottom of the menu — "Saved: default", "Loaded: aggro", "Competitive preset loaded" — so you always know what happened. Keybinds for aimbot, triggerbot, and bhop are captured interactively: click the keybind row, press any key or mouse button, done.

named profiles competitive preset toast notifications keybind capture 120+ settings

Radar & HUD

External Overlay

Custom radar with configurable range, dot size, and background opacity. Shows enemy positions with team-colored dots, facing direction arrows, dead player X markers, grenade positions, and bomb location. Corner placement (TL/TR/BL/BR) with drag-able size.

Spectator list shows who's watching you with optional audio alerts. Velocity graph tracks your movement speed in real-time. The watermark displays FPS, spectator count, and clock in a styled dark box with a red accent line. Custom crosshair with size, gap, thickness, dot, and outline options.

Misc Features

External Optional

Bhop with auto-strafe and speed cap. Anti-flash reduces the flash overlay to a configurable maximum alpha. Glow ESP with pulsing animation and per-team colors. Recoil control with configurable strength and start bullet. FOV changer for wider field of view. Clan tag animator. Key display overlay showing your current inputs.

Hit indicators track real damage by monitoring entity HP changes — not hardcoded values. Kill shots show red markers, headshots show yellow. Damage numbers float upward with smooth animation. The crouch bug is fixed — eye height reads from m_vecViewOffset dynamically instead of using a hardcoded 64 units.
